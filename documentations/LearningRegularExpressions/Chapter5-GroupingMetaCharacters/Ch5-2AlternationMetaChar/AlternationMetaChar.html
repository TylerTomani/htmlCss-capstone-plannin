Alternation Metacharacters

Metacharacter
Meaning
|
Match previous or next expression
  • acts like and "or" operator
  • matches expression on the left or the right
  • leftmost expression gets precedence

examples

  • /apple|orange/

    will match "apple" or "orange"

  • /abc|def|ghi|jkl/

    will match "abc" or "def" or "ghi" or "jkl"

  • /apples(juice|sauce)/

    will match "applejuice" or "applesauce"

    this is not the same as /applejuice|sauce/... USE PARENTHESIS

great way to implement spelling corrections

if we or someone spells weird wrong we can write an expressions

  • /w(ei|ie)rd

    would match "weird" or "wierd"

also great with repititions

  • /(AA|BB|CC){4}/

    would match "AABBAACC" or "CCCCBBBB"

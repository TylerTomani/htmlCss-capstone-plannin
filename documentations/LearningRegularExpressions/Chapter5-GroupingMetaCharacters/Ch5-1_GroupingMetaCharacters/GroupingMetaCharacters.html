Grouping Metacharacters

Metacharacter
Meaning
(
starting grouped expression
)
ending grouped expression
  • /(abc)+ matches "abc" and "abcabcabc
  • /(in)?definite/ matches "indefinite" and "definite"

    • when you make a single character in parenthesis it's the same

  • /run(s)?/ matches "run" and "runs" and would be the same as /runs?/
